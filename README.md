## What is CouponCrowd?

CouponCrowd helps you collect, manage, and use coupons more easily. Instead of searching through old emails, screenshots, printed flyers, or saved posts, you can keep your coupons in one digital wallet and access them when you are ready to shop.

The app is built for everyday savings: store personal coupons, discover public discount codes, share useful offers with others, and get reminded before your coupons expire.

## Key Features

### Save All Your Coupons in One Place

Add coupons, vouchers, discount codes, rebates, and e-coupons to your personal wallet so they are easy to find when you need them.

### Capture Coupons from Different Sources

CouponCrowd helps you save coupons from the places where you already find them:

- Paper coupons
- Screenshots
- Email newsletters
- Social media posts
- Messenger apps
- Shop links
- Online coupon pages

### Get Reminded Before Coupons Expire

CouponCrowd helps you avoid missed savings by reminding you before saved coupons reach their expiry date.

### Find Coupons for Online Shops

Search for shops or share a shop URL with CouponCrowd to find available public coupons, promo codes, and discounts before checkout.

### Share Coupons with Others

Share useful coupons with friends, family, or the wider CouponCrowd community. You can publish public coupons or privately send selected coupons through your favorite messenger, iMessage, or SMS.

### Use QR Codes and Barcodes

CouponCrowd can detect QR codes and barcodes on coupons and display them clearly for in-store scanning.

### Subscribe to Shops

Follow your favorite shops and get notified when new coupons are available.

### Discover Deals by Category

Find shops and offers faster by browsing product categories such as fashion, electronics, sports, food, travel, and more.

### Save Newsletter Coupons

Forward coupon newsletters to CouponCrowd so you can keep your inbox clean while keeping your discounts organized.

### Apply Coupons Faster

Use the built-in browser to open shops and apply coupons more easily.

### Contact Support in the App

Use the integrated support chat to report bugs, suggest improvements, or get help from the CouponCrowd team.
